Asked Questions

This is the page where I answer questions I've received. 

- What programs do you use?

Digital art: Procreate
Traditional art: good ol' #2 pencil and whatever paper is closest
Animation: Toonsquid, sometimes Procreate's built-in animation feature
3D modeling: Nomad Sculpt (sometimes Blender and Blockbench for low-poly models)

- What brushes do you use?

A combination of default Procreate brushes and some downloaded ones. I'm not good at compiling sources of my downloaded brushes, but you can find a Drive folder of the ones I could find here.

- Can I make fanart?

Yes!!! The best way to ensure I'll see it is to send me an email, but you can also DM me on Toyhouse.

- Do you take commissions?

Occasionally, yes! I tend to open them sporadically on Toyhouse and Ko-Fi. 

Where can I find your terms of service?

- Here.

- Can I take inspiration from your work?

Of course! It flatters me to know my stuff can strike a chord in people. 

Inspiration is a natural part of the artistic process, and I'd be nowhere if it hadn't been for the artists whose work inspired mine. Credit is appreciated in cases of heavy referencing, but I won't chase after you for it.

- Can I trace your art?

Yes, if kept private and done strictly for learning purposes. Check out this article for advice on how to properly trace.

- Can I repost your art?

Unless it's a piece you've commissioned or a F2U resource, please do not! My art gets scared when removed from its natural habitat.

- Can I use your art as my icon?

With credit, yes!
