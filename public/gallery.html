FAQ

Hardware? I use an iPad Pro for most of my digital art. I'll sometimes use a Wacom One 8.5x5.3" pen tablet if I'm feeling adventurous.

Software? Mainly Procreate. For animation I typically use Toonsquid or Procreate's built-in animation feature. For 3D modeling it largely depends on case-by-case needs of the project at hand; if it involves rigging I use Blender, but if not I use Nomad Sculpt.

Brushes? See this page!

Traditional supplies? A good ol' mechanical pencil and whatever paper is closest. I mostly work with materials readily available to me and visit local secondhand craft stores if I need anything else.

Other? If you have any specific questions about my supplies, feel free to ask in my comment box or shoot me an email :o)
