Brushes

"What brushes do you use?" is undoubtedly the most frequent art-related question I receive, but because I'm horrible at keeping track of where I download them from, I've never been able to give a satisfactory answer... until NOW!!!! This page exists to remedy that quandary and archive the various custom brushes I use for my digital art.

Keep in mind these will not work across every program. Procreate brushes will likely only work on Procreate, CSP brushes will likely only work on CSP, and so on. There is probably a way to convert a certain brush from one program to another, but unless you're willing to figure out how to do that, you're better off finding (or making) an equivalent for your program of choice.

Brushes I've Made

All of these are available to download on my Kofi for free, though tips are greatly appreciated :-)
